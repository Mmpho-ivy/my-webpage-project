Website Goals and Objectives
•	Main Goal: To create a professional online platform that showcases the Phuluso Nails On brand and its nail services. 
•	Objectives: 
o	Promoting Michelle’s Nail Bar nail services and brand identity 
o	Allowing customers to view a gallery of available designs and sets 
o	Providing information about the technician 
o	Allowing customers to enquire about appointments or custom sets 
•	Key Performance Indicators (KPIs): Success will be measured through online reviews and ratings. 


.KEY Features and Functionality
•	The website will include the following pages: 
•	Homepage (index.html): A landing page introducing Phuluso with a hero image and navigation menu. 
•	About Page (about.html): Information about the technician’s story, mission, and vision.
•	Portfolio/Services Page (services.html): A display of nail sets, including gel, acrylic, and custom nail art. 
•	Enquiry Page (enquiry.html): A form for customers to enquire about bookings or custom designs. 
•	Contact Page (contact.html): Contact information and social media links. 


. Timelines and Milestones
•	Week 1: Project proposal and planning 
•	Week 2: Content research and portfolio image preparation 
•	Week 3: Sitemap and website structure planning 
•	Week 4: HTML development of pages 
•	Week 5: Testing and improvements
