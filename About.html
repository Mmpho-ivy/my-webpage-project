About Us


Michelle’s Nail Bar was founded by michelle also known as Phuluso an independent nail tech business dedicated to modern beauty and creative nail styles. The brand was established to fill a gap in the market for unique, high-quality nail artistry that serves as a place to express your feelings through nails and the colors .

The goal is to create unique, high-quality nail designs that represent modern beauty culture and help people to love themselves through their nails.

our mission is to to create stylish and creative nail art that represents individuality and artistic expression and feelings

which evokes our vision of growing our business into a recognised nail tech business that connects beauty ,feelings,expressions and creativity while reaching a wider local and international client base
