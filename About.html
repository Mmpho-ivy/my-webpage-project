About Us

Michelle’s Nail Bar was founded by Michelle (also known as Phuluso), an independent nail tech business dedicated to modern beauty and creative nail styles. The brand was established to fill a gap in the market for unique, high-quality nail artistry that serves as a place to express your feelings through nails and colors.

Our mission is to create stylish and creative nail art that represents individuality, artistic expression, and emotions. We envision growing into a recognized nail tech business that connects beauty, feelings, and creativity while reaching a wider local and international client base.
